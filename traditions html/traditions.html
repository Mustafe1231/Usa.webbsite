President day

Third Monday in February Americans acknowledge the anniversary of president's day. Presidents day happens around the birthday of Usa most important presidents known as George Washington and Abraham Iincoln although once on this holiday George Washington birthday was only celebrated. Though the US population knows this holiday as president's day, numerous people of the country acknowledge and praise the past president who ruled the country and made America the way it is today. The start of the holiday began around 1880 during George Washington's birthday, honored as a recess. The celebration came to acknowledge both George Washington and Abraham Lincoln's birthday and later all past presidents.
